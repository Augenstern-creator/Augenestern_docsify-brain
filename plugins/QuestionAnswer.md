# 1、问答手风琴

示例:

+ Question 1: Shall I compare thee to a summer's day? +

  Thou art more lovely and more temperate.

+ Question 2: Will we ever meet again? +

  Only if you asked to see me, our meeting would be meaningful to me.

